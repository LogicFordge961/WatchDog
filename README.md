## What is WatchDog?

WatchDog is a free system monitoring tool built by [LogicForge](https://github.com/LogicFordge961) that gives you a real-time view of what's happening on your PC — without slowing it down.

Whether you're gaming, working, or just curious, WatchDog keeps you informed.

---

## Features

- 🖥️ **CPU Usage** — Real-time CPU load monitoring
- 🧠 **Memory / RAM** — See exactly how much memory is in use
- 💾 **Disk Usage** — Track your disk activity and storage
- ⚙️ **Running Processes** — View all active processes at a glance

---

## 🔒 Premium Feature

Want WatchDog to do more than just watch? The **Autopilot** feature lets WatchDog take full control of your system and act on your behalf — automatically managing resources, killing processes, and optimizing performance for you.

> Available as a one-time unlock. Free forever for everything else.
